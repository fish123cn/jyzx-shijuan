### B. 完成句子（共5小题，每小题1分，共5分）
46. **are washed**  
   解析：一般现在时被动语态结构为`am/is/are + 过去分词`，主语`Our hands`是复数，be动词用`are`，`wash`的过去分词是`washed`。

47. **You shouldn’t**  
   解析：`should`的否定形式是`should not`，缩写为`shouldn’t`，后接动词原形。

48. **Where was; built**  
   解析：对地点`in our school`提问用`Where`，原句是一般过去时的被动语态，疑问句将`was`提前，过去分词`built`保持不变。

49. **in different ways**  
   解析：固定短语“以不同的方式”译为`in different ways`。

50. **Henry always shares toys with others.**  
   解析：连词成句遵循“主语+副词+谓语+宾语+状语”结构，`Henry`是主语，`always`是频度副词，`shares`是谓语，`toys`是宾语，`with others`是状语。

### B. 书面表达（15分）
#### 参考范文
Dear Tom,  
I am glad to share with you my experience of facing a setback.  
Last term, I failed my math exam. I felt very sad and wanted to give up. My math teacher noticed my problem and told me that mistakes are part of learning. She helped me find my weak points. Then I made a study plan and practiced math problems every day. Little by little, I made progress.  
From this experience, I learn that setbacks can make us stronger. As long as we face them bravely and keep trying, we will succeed.  
What’s your experience when facing difficulties? Please write back.  
Yours,  
Li Ming
